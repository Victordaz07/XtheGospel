# xTheGospel — Tester Guide

> 10-minute walkthrough to explore the app

---

## What This Is

xTheGospel is a companion app for people learning about the gospel of Jesus Christ. It's designed to feel **safe, calm, and personal**—not like a productivity app.

**Your feedback matters.** We're not testing if buttons work. We're testing if this app makes you feel *accompanied*, not *evaluated*.

---

## Getting Started

1. Open the app in your browser
2. You'll land on the **Home** tab
3. There's no login, no account—your data stays on your device only

---

## 10-Minute Walkthrough

### 1. Explore a Topic (3 min)

1. Tap **Lessons** in the bottom navigation
2. Choose any topic that interests you
3. Read through it at your own pace
4. Notice: there's no "complete" button, no percentage, no timer

**Pay attention to:**
- Do the words feel welcoming or preachy?
- Is it clear what to do next?
- Did anything feel rushed or overwhelming?

---

### 2. Open a Topic Detail (2 min)

1. From the lessons list, tap into a specific topic
2. Scroll through the content
3. Notice the option to save reflections to your journal

**Pay attention to:**
- Is the content easy to read?
- Does the layout feel calm or cluttered?

---

### 3. Write a Journal Entry (2 min)

1. Tap **Journal** in the bottom navigation
2. Write anything—a thought, a question, a feeling
3. Tap **Save Entry**

**Pay attention to:**
- Does this space feel private?
- Would you actually write something personal here?
- Is the empty state helpful or confusing?

---

### 4. Check Your Journey (1 min)

1. Tap **Progress** in the bottom navigation
2. Notice: there are no scores, no streaks, no percentages
3. Just gentle reflections on where you've been

**Pay attention to:**
- Does this feel encouraging or guilt-inducing?
- Is it clear what this page is for?

---

### 5. Export Your Data (1 min)

1. Tap **Profile** in the bottom navigation
2. Scroll to **Data & Privacy**
3. Tap **Export my data**
4. A JSON file will download

**Pay attention to:**
- Do you feel in control of your data?
- Is it clear what's being exported?

---

### 6. Clear Local Data (1 min)

1. In **Profile → Data & Privacy**
2. Tap **Clear local data**
3. Confirm when prompted
4. The app resets to initial state

**Pay attention to:**
- Is the confirmation clear and not scary?
- Does the app feel trustworthy?

---

## What to Pay Attention To

As you explore, notice:

| Question | Why It Matters |
|----------|----------------|
| **Do I feel accompanied or evaluated?** | This should feel like a friend, not a teacher grading you |
| **Do I understand what to do without explanation?** | Good UX is invisible |
| **Would I trust this space with my thoughts?** | Privacy and tone determine trust |
| **Was there any moment of confusion?** | Friction points reveal design problems |
| **What would I never use?** | Honest feedback helps us prioritize |

---

## How to Share Feedback

No forms. No surveys. Just tell us:

1. **Literal quotes** — What did you think or say out loud?
2. **Confusion points** — Where did you pause or feel lost?
3. **Emotional response** — Did anything make you feel peaceful? Anxious? Bored?

You can share feedback via:
- Voice note
- Short text message
- Quick call

---

## What This Is NOT

- ❌ Not a tracking app
- ❌ Not a gamified learning platform
- ❌ Not connected to any church records
- ❌ Not sending your data anywhere

Everything you do stays on your device. You can export it, delete it, or just close the browser.
